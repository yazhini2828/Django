ABOUT US

Coimbatore Institute Of Technology

Coimbatore Institute of Technology is a government-aided autonomous engineering college located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 1956 by V. Rangaswamy Naidu Educational Trust. It is affiliated to Anna University.
